LID World Summit 2026

Organized by CEA-Leti — one of Europe's premier gatherings for semiconductor innovation and deep-tech collaboration.

MINATEC Congress Center, Grenoble, France
June 23–25, 2026
FMC presenting: CxOs Talks, June 23rd

FMC will be joining global semiconductor leaders at the LID World Summit 2026, organized by CEA-Leti. The Summit brings together C-level executives, research institutions, and industry stakeholders advancing the future of semiconductor technology.

FMC will take the stage on June 23rd as part of the CxOs Talks program, sharing FMC's perspective on the role of ferroelectric memory and persistent memory solutions in next-generation AI infrastructure and high-performance computing.

Beyond the keynote, FMC will be engaged in discussions with partners, customers, and industry stakeholders throughout the event — exploring how DRAM+ and CACHE+ can address the memory challenges at the core of AI scale-out, edge compute, and energy-efficient data centre design.

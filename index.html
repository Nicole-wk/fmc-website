Why FMC

The memory breakthrough AI infrastructure demands

Ferroelectric memory combines what DRAM and NAND each do best — finally in a single production-ready solution.

Fast & Persistent

DRAM speed. NAND durability.

FMC's ferroelectric cell architecture delivers sub-10ns access latency with non-volatile data retention — no more trade-offs between speed and persistence.

Energy Efficient

Up to 60% less power.

Designed to meet the energy demands of hyperscale AI data centers. FMC memory dramatically reduces idle and active power consumption versus conventional DRAM.

Manufacturing Ready

Production viable today.

Built on proven semiconductor processes compatible with existing fabs. FMC technology is ready for volume production — not a research roadmap promise.

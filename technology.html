Technology

How FMC Memory Works

Persistent. Fast. Efficient.

FMC™ is advancing memory with ferroelectric (FeFET) technology—combining the speed of DRAM with the persistence of NAND, using a simple, scalable 1T1C cell architecture.

Key Benefits

Breakthrough Memory. Real-World Impact.

FMC memory delivers the performance, persistence, and efficiency modern workloads demand.

DRAM-Class Speed

Nanosecond access with high bandwidth and low latency for real-time workloads.

Persistent by Design

Data is retained at power-off with no refresh—simplifying systems and reducing risk.

Energy Efficient

Lower power per bit at both active and idle—improving efficiency at scale.

Manufacturing Ready

Built on a simple 1T1C cell and standard CMOS processes for easy integration and scale.

Technology Architecture

Simple Cell.
Powerful Results.

FMC's ferroelectric 1T1C cell stores data in the electric polarization of a ferroelectric material—enabling non-volatility with DRAM-like performance.

Company Overview

Built for what's next.

Persistent by design

Data that's always on — without compromise.

Performance at scale

DRAM-speed performance with NAND persistence.

Energy efficient

Lower power, lower TCO, smarter systems.

Built for real-world impact

From AI training to real-time analytics at the edge.

FMC is a leading innovator in persistent memory. Our DRAM+ and CACHE+ solutions combine the performance of DRAM with built-in persistence — delivering real-time speed, data retention, and energy efficiency for AI infrastructure, edge, cloud, and more.

Founded to transform the memory landscape, FMC operates at the intersection of advanced semiconductor design and real-world system requirements. Our technology is purpose-built for the demands of the AI era.
